Connect your Roblox game

Once Don't Blink is published, paste your Universe ID here to pull live stats — visits, player counts, revenue, retention, and session data all in one place.

📍 How to find your Universe ID
1. Go to create.roblox.com
2. Click on your game
3. Look at the URL — it'll be: create.roblox.com/dashboard/creations/experiences/XXXXXXXXX/overview
4. That number is your Universe ID
